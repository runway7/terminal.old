Coming Soon. Signs is a JSON API that lets you painlessly manage your images. It takes care of hosting, resizing and optimizing them for you and serves them off Google's superfast servers.

Development Assistant

Forklift is an assistant that bootstraps your projects, runs your tests while you code, compiles your Javascript and does the things that you can't be bothered with. Open sourced on github.com/runway7/forklift.

Beacon

A Hackers' Blogging Engine

Write your posts in Markdown and push them to GitHub. Beacon will automatically pull, parse, cache and serve them off GAE with the templates that you define. Fork Beacon at github.com/runway7/beacon.
